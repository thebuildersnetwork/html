About

GO SESH is about living life. It is about escaping the mundane, seeking new experiences, and encouraging our users to breakaway from monotonous living. We are a fitness app by definition, but our values at GO SESH are less about getting fit and more about using fitness opportunities to explore the world, meet new people and achieve a healthy and balanced life along the way.
